Alan Rickman

If only life could be a little more tender and art a little more robust.
Alan Sidney Patrick Rickman was an English actor and director.
He was most known for his character of Professor Snape in the Harry Potter series.

    Alan Rickman:

  • 1946 - Born in Hammersmith, London
  • 1965 - Met Rita Horton at the age of 19.
  • 1978 - His first ever movie as an actor released
  • 1995 - Chosen by Empire as one of the 100 Sexiest Stars in film history (No. 34)
  • 2001 - Essayed the role of Porfessor Snape in Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone
  • 2001-2011 - Lived the character of Professor Snape in the entire Harry Potter franchise.
  • 2012 - Married Rita Horton in a private ceremony.
  • 2015 - Suffered a minor stroke which led to the diagnosis of pancreatic cancer.
  • 2016 - Died at the age of 69 in 2016.
